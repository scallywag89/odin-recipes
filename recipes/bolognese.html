Ragu alla Bolognese

A true classic from the city of Bologna

Bolognese sauce is a meat-based sauce in Italian cuisine, typical of the city of Bologna. It is customarily used to dress tagliatelle al rag√π and to prepare lasagne alla bolognese.

Ingredients

For the Sauce

  • 1 stick of celery
  • 1 carrot
  • 1 onion
  • 150g flat pancetta
  • 200g minced beef
  • 200g minced pork
  • 1 glass of quality red wine
  • 3 tbsp good quality tomato paste
  • 200ml chicken stock

To serve

  • 500g Rigatoni
  • Freshly grated parmesan
  • Salt and Pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Finely dice the celery, carrot and onion. Slice pancetta into small lardons. Put ingredients in the pot with a little olive oil and sweat over a medium heat until the vegetables have softened and are starting to brown.
  2. Add the minced meat and break apart into smaller pieces with the back of a wooden spoon. Cook over a medium-high heat until the meat has browned and there is little liquid left in the pot.
  3. Turn the heat to high and add the glass of wine, let bubble until the smell of alcohol has disappeared.
  4. Dissolve the tomato paste in the hot chicken stock, then add to the pot with the rest of the ingredients.
  5. Turn the heat to low, and let simmer for 1.5 hours covered.
  6. Serve over cooked rigatoni, with freshly grated parmesan. Season to taste.
